About

I joined the service in 2003, and along the way I received several awards such as the Enlisted Surface Warfare Specialist certificate (ESWS), the Enlisted Aviation Warfare Specialist certificate (EAWS), Volunteer service medal, and other awards besides while attending college courses and obtaining an arts associates by Excelsior college (2016).

In 2018, after transitioning out of the military I achieved my Bachelors of Computer Science and obtained my first job as a Software developer creating Salesforce marketing emails. I was a contractor for TCS America, and after working there for a year I became the lead email CRM developer.

While working at the Bravern building, where the Microsoft Bings marketing department lives, I got to construct responsive design emails using Salesforce Marketing Cloud (SFMC) for responsive and custom costumer relation experiences.

My daily responsibilities included use browser specific logic to target different email services across multiple devices and platforms, utilized AMP script to specify time and variables for custom targeted marketing emails to suit individuals while utilizing a single template.

This job helped me find out that I love manipulating assets, creating software solutions to display the content the design team provides and suing the Microsoft style guide to develop the content required as expected across as many devices as possible.

Once the contract was over I decided to seek out a job that had front end technologies that I would like to further develop and took an opportunity with Infosys as a contractor working at Apple helping style landing pages for their products.

While still employed I do like to give back to the community and participate in social responsibility inspired community activities such as volunteering teaching kids to code and participating in interviews to help veterans learn about ways to pursue a career in tech just like me. I’m happy in what I do, and I look forward to work in more creative projects.

Portfolio

Bing Ads / Microsoft

(2018 - 2019)

Working at Bing ads for Microsoft as a TCS America contractor meant working on multiple markets that each have individual clients to be reached via custom tailored marketing emails. The solution for Bing is using Salesforce Marketing Cloud (SFMC) content builder to create one email template and then use AMP script to ensure each market clients are plugged in to the body using logic.

On average, I would focus one multiple campaigns during a single sprint; the email marketing campaigns were divided in to several categories; one time like new features from Microsoft or an ongoing monthly webinar with new speakers are just a couple of examples. My job was to manipulate the HTML, CSS, and AMP script (instead of regular JS) to make the vision of the UI team a reality, and a responsive reality that qualified under the Microsoft style guides as on brand at the same time.

The most important part of my job was to make sure the UI was correct even when the screens were of smaller devices or across email services: after a while I was asked to help other teams when they had problems displaying their assets because I have a good handle on basic CSS and like to take my time to figure out the most compatible solutions for as many users as possible.

Technologies: Salesforce (SFMC), content builder, Web-Smart (EPNY), CSS, AMP-script, Marketo, SQL, Journey-builder, Automation Studio, HTML (emails are table based and not HTML5).
